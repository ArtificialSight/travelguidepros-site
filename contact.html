Response Time

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent requests or last-minute bookings, please call our main office number for immediate assistance.

When contacting us, please include:

  • Your preferred destination(s)
  • Travel dates (even if flexible)
  • Number of travelers in your group
  • Special interests or requirements
  • Your budget range (if applicable)

This information helps us provide you with the most relevant recommendations and accurate pricing for your travel experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance, though earlier booking is preferred for popular destinations and peak travel seasons.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes! We offer special pricing for groups of 6 or more. Contact us for detailed group rates and custom packages.

What languages do your guides speak?

Our guides speak multiple languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, and many local languages depending on the destination.

What's your cancellation policy?

We offer flexible cancellation policies. Free cancellation up to 24 hours before your tour, with weather-related cancellations fully refunded.
